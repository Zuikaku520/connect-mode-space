# Experiment Roadmap

## Stage 1
Verify whether different connection modes produce different output dynamics.

## Stage 2
Test whether weight-only tuning can approximate those differences.

## Stage 3
Test whether combined modes open new dynamical regions.

## Stage 4
Test robustness under repeated noisy runs.

## Stage 5
Use ablation to determine whether higher-order combinations collapse back toward parent modes.

## Stage 6
Construct synergy maps over parameter space.

## Stage 7
Identify and refine local synergy hotspots.
